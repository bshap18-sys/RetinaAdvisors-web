What you're up against

Business email compromise

An email that looks exactly like it came from your CFO, asking for a wire transfer today.

Account takeover

Someone logs into a real customer's account and drains it before anyone notices.

Vendor fraud

A fake invoice from a vendor you already trust, just with new bank details.

Deepfake calls

A voice that sounds exactly like your boss, asking you to do something you'd normally question.

Internal & employee fraud

The fraud nobody wants to think about, coming from inside your own team.

Payment & money movement fraud

Funds get redirected mid-transfer, or moved out the second an account's compromised.

Questions

How long does a gap analysis take?

Most of the time it's a focused review, not a months-long audit. We'll figure out the timeline together on the first call, once we understand your business.

Do we need to already suspect a problem?

No. Most of the value is finding exposure before anything happens. Waiting until something feels wrong usually means it's already too late.

Are we too small for this?

If you're growing and nobody's thinking about fraud full-time, you're exactly who this is for. You don't need to be a big company to be exposed.

Is this the same as a cybersecurity audit?

No. We focus on the human and financial side of risk, the scams, the vendor fraud, the account takeovers, not network security or IT infrastructure.
